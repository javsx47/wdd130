Slace Site Plan

Site Name

Slace - Shoelaces to go places

Pages

The website will be focused on 3 pages, the home site, the shoelace sizing guide, and the contact us page every Shoelace will have a price and its respective review to transmit trust and promote the item, the sizing guid will be pretty straight forward with a chart and a video reference and lastly the contact us page will have some contact details of our company and a frequently asked questions table.

Images that will be used

Site Purpose

Make the shopping of shoelaces easy and enjoyable

Audience

The audience is gonna be focused on young and fashionable people that like to change style and notice little things just like shoe laces, we will specialize in pairing with top Nike shoe models and some other notorious brands. We will focuse on answering questions such as how much will it cost? how much is the shipping cost? Can I retrurn my item? Warranty?

I'm gonna keep the colors simple because the main objective of the website is highlight the colors of the shoelaces so I will use black, white, and maybe a light tone of grey.
